About Me

I'm Cassidy Fowler, an artist based in Northern New York. I love creating all sorts of media from illustrations and graphics to photo and video! I am currently pursuing my bachelors in Digital Arts & sciences, with a minor in Communication.

I have taken courses in illustration, infographics, photo, video, typography, and more. I also have paid and unpaid experience in marketing and graphic design. This site serves as a place for me to showcase works that I'm proud of!
